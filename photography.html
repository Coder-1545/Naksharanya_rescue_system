Ethical Wildlife Photography Tips

Respect Wildlife

Observe from a distance. Never approach animals too closely for the shot.

No Feeding

Feeding wildlife disturbs their behavior and puts them in danger.

Be Patient

The best shots come to those who wait quietly and respect the surroundings.
